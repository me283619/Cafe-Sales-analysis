## 💡 Key Business Insights

Based on the dashboard analysis, here are the main takeaways:

1. **Top Revenue Drivers:** * **Juice** and **Salad** are the best-selling items, generating the highest revenue (over $36K combined).
   * **Recommendation:** Consider creating a "Healthy Combo" featuring both to increase the average order value.

2. **Seasonal Trends:**
   * There is a significant peak in sales and order counts during **February** (reaching 1,187 orders).
   * **Insight:** This might be due to a specific event or promotion. We should investigate what caused this spike to replicate it in other months.

3. **Payment Preferences:**
   * **Digital Wallets** are the most preferred payment method (**54%**), followed equally by Cash and Credit Cards (23% each).
   * **Action:** Ensure the digital payment system is always optimized and consider loyalty rewards for digital wallet users.

4. **Service Type:**
   * **70%** of customers prefer **In-Store** dining compared to 30% for Takeaway.
   * **Insight:** The physical space and ambiance of the cafe are crucial for customer satisfaction.
